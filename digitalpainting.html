Abstraction

(2021), Procreate

This series is a passion project that explores lines, textures, and movement in an abstract way. It is meant to move the viewer's eye by way of textures and chromatic arribriation.

Pink David

2021, Procreate

Exploring monochromatic tones of pink, this piece takes the iconic Stature of David and adapts him into a painterly-based style to give him a new way to be viewed.

For Your Pleasure

2022, Procreate

This project was a means of exploring different properties in Procreate, and demonstrates the learning process of this digital medium.

Sea Tattoo

2022, Procreate

As a commission for a tattoo, this piece displays realism in digital painting by way of monochromatic shades of gray.

(un)done

2021, Procreate

This piece was made for a collaborative zine that notes on the climate crisis. The zebra's iconic stripes are melting off as a result of the climate crisis, and is in turn showing how we are "doing" what cannot be "undone".
